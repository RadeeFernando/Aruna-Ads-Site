    • CNC CUTTING & ENGRAVING

    CNC (Computer Numerical Control) cutting and engraving are advanced manufacturing processes that use computer-controlled machines to precisely cut, carve, or etch materials.

    • ~ CNC Cutting involves using a CNC machine equipped with tools like routers, lasers, or plasma cutters to cut through materials such as wood, metal, plastic, and composites. The machine follows a pre-programmed design, ensuring high precision and repeatability for complex shapes, patterns, and detailed cuts. It’s widely used in industries like aerospace, automotive, and sign making.

    • ~ CNC Engraving is the process of etching or carving intricate designs, text, or patterns onto the surface of a material. Similar to cutting, CNC engraving uses a rotating tool, but instead of cutting through, it typically creates shallow grooves or marks. It’s commonly used for adding logos, serial numbers, decorative elements, or detailed artwork on items like metal plates, jewelry, or wood products.

    Both processes offer efficiency, high precision, and the ability to create highly intricate designs with minimal human intervention. They are ideal for mass production as well as custom, one-off creations.
